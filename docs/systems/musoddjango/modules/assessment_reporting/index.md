### Assessment Reporting (MSL) Overview/Brief
- The AssessmentReporting module is MUSOD's analytics and reporting layer for assessment quality, grading patterns, and rubric-consistency oversight. It consumes grading data (primarily from MySimLab), computes statistical indicators, caches those results in reporting tables, and presents dashboards plus export-ready outputs for academic and operational decision support.
